Game Features

  • 🌱 Real-time crop planting, growth, and harvesting
  • 🛠️ Unlock tool upgrades to harvest faster
  • 🌾 Buy new plots and expand your farm empire
  • 💸 Trade resources and invest in automation
  • 🎉 Earn achievements and seasonal event rewards

How to Play

  1. Begin with a small plot and basic tools.
  2. Click to plant seeds and wait for them to grow.
  3. Harvest crops to earn coins.
  4. Upgrade tools to improve efficiency.
  5. Buy new land and automate your farm.
