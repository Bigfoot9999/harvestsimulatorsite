Game Overview

Harvest Simulator is a free, browser-based farming game where you can plant crops, harvest them for coins, and expand your farm. No downloads required!

Game Controls

  • Click to plant seeds and harvest crops.
  • Use the shop to buy upgrades and new land.
  • Automate tasks with upgrades to earn coins passively.

Game Features

  • 🌱 Real-time crop planting, growth, and harvesting
  • 🛠️ Unlock tool upgrades to harvest faster
  • 🌍 Expand your farm with new land and crops
  • 🏆 Complete challenges for extra rewards

Game Tips

  • Upgrade your tools early to harvest faster.
  • Reinvest your coins into better crops and land.
  • Complete challenges for extra rewards.
