Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Harvest Simulator collects and uses your data.

We do not collect any personal data from players. We may collect anonymous usage data through cookies and third-party analytics to improve the game experience.

Use of Information

Any anonymous data collected is used solely to enhance the gameplay experience, fix bugs, and understand how players interact with Harvest Simulator.

Cookies

Harvest Simulator uses cookies to store game progress locally in your browser. No personal data is stored or transmitted.

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics for anonymous traffic analysis. These services have their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to clear cookies and site data in your browser at any time, which will reset your game progress.

Children's Privacy

Harvest Simulator is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy
