The Best Crops to Plant in Harvest Simulator

By Bigfoot Games | July 2025

Choosing the right crops is essential for maximizing your profits and progressing quickly in Harvest Simulator. Some crops grow faster, while others yield more coins per harvest. Here’s a breakdown of the best crops to plant at every stage of the game.

Top Early-Game Crops

  • Wheat: Fast-growing and reliable for beginners.
  • Carrots: Slightly longer growth time but higher payout.
  • Lettuce: Quick to harvest and great for building up coins.

Mid-Game Crop Choices

  • Corn: Balanced growth and profit, ideal for expanding farms.
  • Tomatoes: Good yield and used in some achievements.
  • Strawberries: Take longer but offer big rewards.

Late-Game & Special Crops

  • Pumpkins: High value, especially during events.
  • Blueberries: Great for automation and passive income.
  • Event Crops: Always plant event crops for unique rewards!
“Mixing fast and slow crops keeps my farm busy and my coins growing.” – Veteran Player

Tips for Crop Management

  • Rotate crops to keep your plots productive at all times.
  • Upgrade your tools to speed up harvesting of high-yield crops.
  • Watch for seasonal events that boost certain crop rewards.

Conclusion

Experiment with different crops to find the best strategy for your play style. Happy planting!
